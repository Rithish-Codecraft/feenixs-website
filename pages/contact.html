Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What kind of partnerships are you looking for?

We're open to various types of partnerships including research collaborations, technology integrations, academic partnerships, and investment opportunities. If you're working on innovative AI projects or have resources that could help advance our mission, we'd love to hear from you.

How can I contribute to Feenixs projects?

You can contribute by joining our community, participating in discussions, contributing to open-source projects, sharing research ideas, or collaborating on specific initiatives. We also welcome feedback and suggestions that can help improve our work.

Do you offer internships or job opportunities?

As we grow, we're looking for talented individuals passionate about AI and innovation. Currently, we offer internship opportunities and are building our core team. Reach out with your resume and areas of interest to explore potential collaborations.

How quickly can I expect a response?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters or partnership opportunities, we prioritize quick responses. Complex technical questions may take additional time for thorough review.

Can I visit your facility or meet in person?

We welcome virtual meetings and collaborations. For in-person visits, please contact us in advance to schedule appointments. We also participate in AI conferences and events where you can meet our team.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

Please include your background, specific area of interest, proposed collaboration idea, and what you hope to achieve. For technical inquiries, include relevant details about your project or research. The more specific you are, the better we can assist you.
